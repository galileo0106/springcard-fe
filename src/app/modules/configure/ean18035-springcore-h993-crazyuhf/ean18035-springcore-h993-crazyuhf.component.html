Whatever the settings in bits 5-4 of bytes 1 and 2, the registers holding the two passwords (02FE and 02FF) can never be read back, and the Inventory and License registers (0200 to 020F) are always readable. When the "Protect admin key and sensitive registers" bit in bytes 1 (Local) and 2 (Remote) is set, the HostCommAdminKey and the registers 02F0 to 02FF (including this Access Conditions register itself) can be changed only after an authentication using HostCommAdminKey.
